Our Services

Livestock Quarantine & Disease Prevention

Our quarantine facility is designed to ensure that all exported livestock is free from infectious diseases. We follow strict biosecurity measures and conduct veterinary health screenings before export.

  • Regular health inspections by certified veterinarians.
  • Disease detection and prevention through laboratory testing.
  • Compliance with global export health regulations.

Research & Disease Control

We conduct scientific research on livestock diseases prevalent in the Horn of Africa to:

  • Identify and prevent outbreaks that could impact livestock trade.
  • Improve livestock health through data-driven insights.
  • Develop strategies for sustainable livestock farming.

Training Programs

We offer short-term training for:

  • Veterinarians.
  • Laboratory technicians.
  • Livestock traders and handlers.

Our training programs focus on modern quarantine procedures, disease management, and export regulations.

Livestock Market Information

We provide real-time livestock market data, helping traders and investors make informed decisions. Our data includes:

  • Price trends for different livestock types.
  • Export regulations in various countries.
  • Supply and demand forecasts for international markets.
