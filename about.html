About us

Welcome to SILQC

Saudi International Livestock Quarantine Center (SILQC) is Somaliland’s largest and most advanced livestock quarantine facility. Covering 2 km², our center is located 3 km from Berbera City, strategically positioned to facilitate seamless livestock export worldwide.

Founded in 2025, SILQC is a privately developed facility, regulated and supervised by the Government of Somaliland through the Ministry of Livestock and Rural Development. Our goal is to ensure disease-free, high-quality livestock exports by maintaining the highest standards in animal health and biosecurity.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of livestock quarantine services in the Horn of Africa, ensuring safe and disease-free global trade.

Our Mission

We provide bio-secure quarantine services, protecting animal health, facilitating smooth trade, and upholding the highest welfare standards.

Our Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Sustainability, Collaboration, Animal Welfare, Responsibility.
